Experience the diversity of art, music, and culinary traditions at Art.Instepanavan.am

Welcome to art.instepanavan.am!

art.instepanavan.am is an art, music, and culinary festival taking place at the end of August in Stepanavan. The festival celebrates diversity and encourages attendees to discover and enjoy new art, music, and culinary experiences. Whether you are a musician, artist, or culinary expert, we invite you to apply to participate in our event and share your talents with a diverse audience.

Musicians

If you are a musician with a passion for folk or international folk music, we encourage you to apply to perform at art.instepanavan.am. We are interested in all types of music and welcome applications from solo artists, bands, and other types of musical groups. To apply, please fill out the form at http://form.com/artinstepanavan/musicians with information about yourself and your music. Be sure to include your name, email, genre, and a description of your music and performance style, as well as links to examples of your work.

Artists

If you are an artist interested in creating and showcasing your work at art.instepanavan.am, we encourage you to apply to participate in our event. We are looking for talented artists to create a range of public art installations, street arts, and interactive art pieces. To apply, please fill out the form at http://form.com/artinstepanavan/artists with information about yourself and your art. Be sure to include your name, email, and a description of your art and style, as well as links to examples of your work. We welcome artists of all types and styles, and encourage you to apply and share your artistic talents with us.

Culinary Experts

If you are a chef or culinary expert with a passion for local or international cuisines, we encourage you to apply to participate in art.instepanavan.am. We are committed to showcasing a diverse range of cuisines at the festival, and are looking for talented culinary experts to share their culinary creations with attendees. To apply, please fill out the form at http://form.com/artinstepanavan/culinaryexperts with information about yourself and your culinary expertise. Be sure to include your name, email, cuisine(s) of expertise, and a description of your culinary style and any notable accomplishments or experience, as well as links to examples of your work.

Open Stage/Open Mic

Art.Instepanavan.am is all about celebrating diversity and encouraging attendees to discover and share their talents with the community. That's why we are excited to offer an open stage/open mic event for attendees to share their music, comedy, poetry, or any other type of performance. If you have a talent you want to share, we encourage you to sign up and join us on the stage!

How to Participate

To sign up for the open stage/open mic event at art.instepanavan.am, please fill out the form at http://form.com/artinstepanavan/openstage with your name, email, and a brief description of your performance. Spaces are limited, so be sure to sign up early to secure your spot on the stage. Performers will be selected on a first-come, first-served basis, so don't wait to sign up!

Guidelines

We encourage all types of performances at the open stage/open mic event, but we do have a few guidelines to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for all attendees:

  • Performances should be no longer than 10 minutes in length.
  • Performances should be appropriate for all ages.
  • Amplification is allowed if it is connected via a single jack cable. No other special equipment is allowed - all performers must use the provided sound system and.

Open Air Theater

Art.Instepanavan.am is excited to bring the beauty of nature and the magic of theater together with our open air theater event. Set against the stunning backdrop of Stepanavan, this unique theater experience will transport attendees to another world as they watch live performances under the stars. The open air theater event is free and open to all attendees, so don't miss this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to experience theater in a whole new way at Art.Instepanavan.am!

We look forward to seeing your work and hope to see you at art.instepanavan.am!
